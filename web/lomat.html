About The Land of Mists and Trails (In Progress)
Free Web Demo

LOMAT is the tale of Eirikr Kharun, who discovers himself to be the foretold Nowhere Guide.

The sqwawking seagulls of his land screech a sad story of directions gone meaningless because of the Titan Fenrir, of being spooky ghosts with no idea where their final resting place anymore is. It's up to Eirikr to use his trusty viking boat wagon to transport the gulls across the Land through treacherous mists to seek out their homes.

Core gameplay includes a simple, nostalgic hunting mechanic, resource and party management and a heavy emphasis on exploration.

Key Features


Completely Procedural Maps: Towns shift, not only across broad distances, but even within their own borders.

NPC Dialogue: Gulls and more can converse with the Nowhere Guide to provide direction, narrative and secrets.

Build Your Own Tombstone: peperony and chease
