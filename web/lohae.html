LOHAE is the tale of Zawhei Bacama, who discovers herself to be the foretold Life Reaper.

The thwapping beavers of her Land excitedly explain that they have been gnawing down each tree as it grows to avoid awakening the Titan, Nidhogg. They hope their prophesied hero can manage to grow the trees without ruining everything forever!

Breed trees together to unlock new types of fruits, and make progress on the mysterious journey to collecting enough Essences for ... hrrrm... is that...supposed to be happening? There's a flash light in this game? What happens if you...

Key Features


Breed Procedural Trees: Each tree has a simple genetic code that can be combined in various ways with other trees to form offspring.

Over 85 Fruit Forms: Collect various colors of over 85 base fruit types, from apples to skulls.

Definitely no Secrets???: Unless you go digging, you can merely enjoy this relaxing tree breeding game. Who even cares about Nidhogg, honestly?
